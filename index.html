注意安全

1. 进入厂区请佩戴安全帽，禁止在非吸烟区吸烟。

2. 操作设备前请确认安全规程，避免违规操作。

3. 发现安全隐患请立即联系管理人员，切勿自行处理。

4. 紧急情况请按应急按钮，并沿安全通道撤离。

Attention to Safety

1. Please wear a safety helmet when entering the factory area. Smoking is prohibited in non-smoking areas.

2. Confirm safety regulations before operating equipment to avoid illegal operations.

3. If you find potential safety hazards, contact management immediately. Do not handle them yourself.

4. In case of emergency, press the emergency button and evacuate along the safe passage.
